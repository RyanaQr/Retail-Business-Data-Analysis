
## Business Problem Solutions:

The following analytical business scenarios were covered in this repository:

- Identifying high-value customers
- Finding clients without invoices
- Invoice trend analysis
- Product price comparison
- Customer segmentation
- Payment method analysis
- Ranking customers based on invoice totals

## Learning Outcomes:

By working through this project, you can practice:

- Writing efficient SQL queries
- Understanding relational databases
- Performing analytical business queries
- Using joins and aggregations
- Working with advanced SQL concepts
- Solving Data Analyst problems
